The Best Movies According to Tom

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Prison Break

I enjoy how drama movies focus on the personal development and choices of their characters. The acting in drama movies is usually top-notch, with actors delivering complex and nuanced performances that bring the story to life. I love seeing talented actors at their best.

A Walk to Remember

I love how romantic movies highlight the deep emotional connections between characters, making me believe in the power of love and the idea that soulmates can exist.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away beautifully captures Chihiro's emotional growth. Watching her go from a scared, uncertain girl to a brave, resourceful young woman is inspiring and relatable for viewers of all ages.
